Do you ship internationally?▼
Yes! We ship worldwide. International shipping typically takes 7-12 business days. Free shipping on orders over $100. All products are carefully packaged to ensure they arrive safely.
Can I visit your studio?▼
Absolutely! Our studio in Pune is open Monday-Saturday. We offer guided tours and botanical workshops. Walk-ins welcome, or you can book a private session for groups of 4+.
Do you offer wholesale pricing?▼
Yes, we partner with retailers, spas, and boutiques worldwide. Email wholesale@flamora.com with your business details to get started with a custom wholesale account.
Are your products sustainable?▼
100%! All our ingredients are ethically sourced from sustainable farms. Our packaging is biodegradable, and we use only natural, cruelty-free materials in all our products.
